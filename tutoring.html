Build Our Bridge Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to offering free tutoring in English, Math, Science, Coding, Music, and Art to underprivileged students from countries all over the world. I volunteered as a tutor for the organization throughout high school, and this summer, I worked with two other former leaders to redesign and build a new website for them.

The aim of this project was to enhance usability and improve the overall user experience, focusing on time-keeping and messaging features for students and tutors, while also considering scalability and ease of maintenance for the future. We are currently in the development stage of the project, using React and Django to implement the website.
